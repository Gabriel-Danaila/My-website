  • Adaptive Cruise Control

    This feature will keep you at a minimum pre-set distance from the vehicle in front. Should the distance reduce, the system gradually slows your car down and eventually the brakes will be applied if necessary. If the distance increases again, the car will gradually accelerate until you return to your chosen cruising speed

  • Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection

    When the possibility of collision with either a cyclist or pedestrian is detected the driver is alerted with audio and visual warnings and brake assistance is activated, if necessary the brakes are engaged automatically.

A WORLD OF YOUR OWN

An expansive rear cabin of Grace White and Iceland Moss leather makes for a sumptuous sanctuary, where escapism is the main objective. Personal legacies are built beneath the Shooting Star Headliner — a mesmerising celestial display crafted from hundreds of fibre-optic lights.

CRYSTAL CLEAR AUDIO

In this acoustic sanctum, sound is made to be immersed in. The Bespoke Audio system is tuned by expert ears, ensuring only the richest studio-quality sounds break through the silence.

RAISE A GLASS

The rear seat Coolbox houses hand-blown flutes and chilled bottles of your favourite refreshments, kept at the perfect temperature for crisp sips. Enjoy a celebratory glass by yourself or toast a special occasion with your inner circle.

RELISH THE SILENCE

Enjoy a supremely quiet journey where your thoughts are the only things heard. Or rather, transform the rear cabin into a venue for elevated entertainment free from outside distractions.

HIDDEN THEN REVEALED

A Rolls-Royce umbrella is discreetly stored within the door, which is fitted with a drainage channel to clear away excess water. From light showers to heavy downpours, passengers are suitably equipped with this concealed accessory.
